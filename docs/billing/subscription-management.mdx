# Billing, Plans & Subscription Management

## Available Subscription Plans

Bacon currently offers 2 tiers:

### 1. Paid Trial Plan – $9.99

A 7-day trial designed to help you experience Bacon without restrictions.

**What you get:**

- Full access to all features
- 50 credits included
- Auto-upgrades to the Pro plan after 7 days (unless cancelled before renewal)

Perfect for users who want to test Bacon's capabilities before committing.

### 2. Pro – $99/month

The full-power plan for professional creators.

The complete Bacon experience - ideal for creators, marketers, and businesses producing content regularly.

**What you get:**

- Full access to all features
- 500 credits every month
- Monthly renewal
- Can cancel anytime (plan stays active until the cycle ends)

## Plan Rules & Management

### Can I downgrade?

❌ No.

You can cancel your subscription, but downgrades are currently not supported.

### How cancellations work

- You can cancel at any time.
- Your plan stays active until the end of your billing cycle.

## Billing Summary

- All paid plans renew monthly.
- Trial auto-upgrades to Pro unless cancelled.
- Plan credits expire monthly; purchased credits last 90 days.
- Cancellations allowed anytime; no downgrades.
