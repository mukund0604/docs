# Billing, Plans & Subscription Management

## Available Subscription Plans

Bacon offers **2 Pro plans** with full access to all features.

### Pro Monthly — **\$29 / month**

Perfect for creators who want flexibility.

**What you get:**

- Full access to all features
- **150 credits every month**
- Billed monthly
- Cancel anytime (plan remains active until the billing cycle ends)

### Pro Yearly — **\$19 / month (Billed Monthly)**

_Best value_

Commit yearly and save more each month.

**What you get:**

- Full access to all features
- **150 credits every month**
- **\$19/month, billed monthly** with yearly commitment
- Cancel anytime (plan remains active until the billing cycle ends)

## What’s Included in Pro

- Transform product URLs into polished images & videos instantly
- Multi-platform, auto-resized creatives (1:1, 16:9, 9:16, 4:5)
- Generate multiple variations for A/B testing
- AI-powered edits for fast changes
- Brand DNA for consistent, on-brand creatives
- Animate static images into videos
- Cinematic-quality videos with Veo 3
- Create influencer-style & UGC videos with AI avatars
- Pro mode for granular creative control

## Plan Rules & Billing

- Credits reset every month
- Unused plan credits expire monthly
- Purchased add-on credits last 90 days
- No downgrades supported
- Cancellation allowed anytime

## Plan Rules & Management

### Can I downgrade?

❌ yes

You can upgrade to another plan at any time. However, downgrades are not supported while a plan is active. Once you switch plans, you can change to another plan only after the current plan ends.

### How cancellations work

- You can cancel at any time.
- Your plan stays active until the end of your billing cycle.

## Billing Summary

- All paid plans renew monthly.
- Trial auto-upgrades to Pro unless cancelled.
- Plan credits expire monthly; purchased credits last 90 days.
- Cancellations allowed anytime; no downgrades.
