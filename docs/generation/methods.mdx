# Methods of Generation : How to Generate (URL & Upload Methods)

Bacon gives you two ultra-fast ways to generate images and videos:

1. Drop your product URL
2. Upload your product images manually

Both methods produce studio-quality creatives that match your brand identity automatically.

## METHOD 1: Generate Using a URL (Recommended Method ⭐)

### Step 1: Choose the Type of Ad You Want to Create

Once you land on Bacon’s dashboard, choose the type of ad you want to create:

Image Ads / UGC Ads / Video Ads / Influencer Ads / Veo3 Video

→ Start prompting!

### Step 2: Paste Your Product Link

Copy your website or product page URL and paste it into Bacon’s input box.
Also describe the type of ad you want to create.

Bacon instantly scans your page and generates high-quality ads based on your product.

### Step 3: Choose Your Best Fit

Bacon analyzes your brand and identifies the best-fit target audience for your ad.

Simply select the audience you want to target, and your ad will be tailored specifically to them.

### Step 4: Generate Your Ads

Hit Generate.

In 10–20 seconds, you'll see:

- An on-brand image/video/ UCG/influencer ad
- With copy, CTAs, typography, and brand elements already matched
- Auto-resized versions for Meta, TikTok, Google, YouTube, etc. based on your choosing

### Step 5: Customize

Select an image to access the **AI Edit** options below.

### 5.1 AI Edit

Use AI Edit to customize your creative exactly the way you want:

- Swap backgrounds
- Change text, text color, font, and more
- Add new product angles
- Reposition elements
- Modify layouts
- Insert props and more

### 5.2 Variations

Create multiple versions of your creative for your brand.

**Steps:**

1. Select the image you want to create variations for
2. Choose how many variations you want (1 to 4)
3. Instantly generate different variations of the selected image

### 5.3 Resize

Adjust your image to different aspect ratios to fit various platforms.

**Available aspect ratios:**

- 9:16
- 3:4
- 1:1
- 3:2
- 16:9
- 4:3

Select the size that best fits your use case.

### 5.4 Animate

Turn your image into a motion-based video ad with subtle animations.

**Steps:**

1. Click **Animate**
2. Select your preferred aspect ratio
3. Choose the quality (Basic or High)
4. Click **Animate** to generate the animated creative

The creative is your playground, you can edit any element generated as you'd like!

### Step 6: Export Your Ad

Choose export options:

- PNG
- JPG

Your creatives are now launch-ready!

## METHOD 2: Generate Ads Using Product Uploads

If you don't have a URL, or want to use photos not on your website, use the Upload workflow.

### Step 1: Upload Your Product Images

By clicking on the attach icon, upload images of:

- Product photos
- Lifestyle photos
- Studio shots
- Raw phone images

Bacon removes backgrounds instantly and identifies:

- Product shape
- Material
- Texture
- Branding elements

### Step 3: Type a Prompt

Examples:

- "Make a clean e-commerce ad with modern gradients."
- "Lifestyle kitchen setting for a high-end cookware brand."

You can be as simple or detailed as you want!

### Step 4: Generate, Review and Edit as usual

You get: A Clean and professional ad, and you can choose to create multiple variations. The same full editor applies!
