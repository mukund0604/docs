# Step-by-Step Onboarding Guide

When you log in for the first time, Bacon takes you through a fast onboarding flow to understand your brand identity and create accurate, high-performing creatives.

Here's each step:

## 1. Add Your Website URL

Once you’ve logged in with your email, you’ll be prompted to enter your brand details so Bacon can generate on-brand creatives.

1. **Company name**
2. **Company website URL**

This helps Bacon understand your brand and tailor creatives automatically.

## 3. Brand Intelligence Scan

Bacon scans your site for:

- Logo
- Typography
- Colors
- Visual style
- Brand Voice

## 4. Review Your Brand Profile

You'll see a summary of everything Bacon detected.

You can also edit - add or remove any of the details collected for the Logo, Typography, Color palette, Visual and layout standards, and Tone, Personality, Key phrases and Messaging gathered under Brand Voice.

## 5. Final Confirmation

Your workspace is now ready. Welcome to Bacon!
