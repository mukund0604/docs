# Profile & Settings

When you click your Profile Icon at the bottom-left corner of the dashboard, you'll see options to:

- Switch to Dark Mode
- Access Settings
- Contact Support
- Log Out

Inside Settings, you'll find several sections:

## 1. Profile Page

Manage everything related to your personal and business details.

You can edit:

**Personal Information**

- Profile photo (JPG, PNG, GIF, up to 20MB)
- Full name
- Email address
- Company name
- Job title

Customize your workspace experience.

## 2. Subscription Page

A dedicated page to manage your billing plan.

Here, you can:

- View your current plan
- Upgrade your plan
- Manage your subscription
- Purchase additional credits
- View plan benefits

## 3. Usage Page

Your complete credit breakdown lives here.

You can view:

**Usage Filters:**

- Today
- Yesterday
- Last 7 days
- Last 30 days
- Last 90 days

**What you'll see in the usage table:**

- Date
- User
- Tool used
- Action performed
- Credits consumed (or credits added via top-up)

You can also Export a CSV of your entire usage history.

This page also includes Buy Credits → slider → Stripe checkout (already documented in your Credits article).

## 4. Invoices Page

When you open this page, you are redirected to your Stripe Billing Portal, where you can:

- View your active plan
- Access all past invoices
- Update billing information
- Add or remove payment methods
- View your next billing date
- Cancel your subscription

Everything related to your payments lives inside Stripe for complete transparency.
